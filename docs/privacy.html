Privacy Policy Global Exchange · Last updated: July 21, 2026 Short version: Global Exchange does not collect, store, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no tracking, no analytics, and no ads. Information we collect We do not collect any personal information. Global Exchange has no user accounts and does not ask for your name, email address, location, or any other identifying information. Data stored on your device Your preferences — the currencies you select, your base currency, and the most recently downloaded exchange rates — are stored locally on your device using Apple's standard on-device storage. This data never leaves your device and is removed when you delete the app. Network requests To show up-to-date exchange rates, the app downloads publicly available rate data from a third-party currency data provider (the Currency API by Fawaz Ahmed, delivered via public content delivery networks). These requests contain only the currency you are viewing. No personal information is transmitted with them. Third-party tracking Global Exchange does not use any advertising, analytics, or tracking frameworks, and does not track you across apps or websites. Children's privacy The app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Changes to this policy If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
